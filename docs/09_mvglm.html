Multivariate GLM (mvGLM) for community composition

Hypothesis predictor variables:

  • Cover resources:
    • Vegetation cover
    • Litter cover
    • Coarse woody debris volume
  • Food resources:
    • Forb biomass
    • Shrub biomass
    • Tree biomass
  • Other:
    • Fire severity categories
    • Random effect - Principle Components of Neighborhood Matrix (PCNM)
